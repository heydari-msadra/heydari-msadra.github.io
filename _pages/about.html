About Me

PhD Student in Economics

I am currently a first year PhD student in Economics at the Adam Smith Business School, University of Glasgow, where I work under the supervision of Professor Richard Dennis.

My research is primarily focused on Industry Dynamics and the strategic interactions that shape market structure and innovation.

Before starting my PhD, I completed an MRes in Economics (2023–2025) at the University of Glasgow. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering with a Minor in Economics from Sharif University of Technology (2018–2023). My academic background bridges computational methods and economic theory, which I apply to studying firm behaviour and dynamic markets.
